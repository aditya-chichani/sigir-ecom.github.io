Rakuten Data Challenge

Taxonomy Classification for eCommerce-scale Product Catalogs

Overview

SIGIReCom workshop will host a machine learning data challenge targeting taxonomy classification for eCommerce-scale product catalogs.

We will provide data including several million product titles and descriptions from the Rakuten.com catalog featuring thousands of tree-structured labels. The cataloging of product listings through taxonomy categorization is a fundamental problem for any eCommerce marketplace, with applications ranging from personalized search & recommendation to query understanding.

Advances in this area of research have been limited due to the lack of real data from actual commercial catalogs. The challenge presents several interesting research aspects due to the intrinsic noisy nature of the product labels, the size of modern eCommerce catalogs, and the typical unbalanced data distribution.
